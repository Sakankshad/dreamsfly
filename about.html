ABOUT US

Let Your Dreams Fly


Dreamsfly is an Indian MBBS Consultancy that provides the best in class admission consulting services to medical aspirants abroad. Our in-depth analysis, counseling and guidance services give you a brilliant head start to make through the complex and confusing process of choosing the right college in a different country.

We decipher your goals and guide you throughout the process with small steps to achieve them. In India, there are close to 49000 MBBS seats for approximately 12 lakh aspirants. In fact with the prevailing reservation policies and reservation of seats for state-level candidates, there has been a sticky situation for candidates from a number of states in India. Candidates from these states need to have a prior idea about the best strategy they could adopt to achieve the best results and do not miss on their opportunities of admission in best-fit colleges abroad as per their academic and financial suitability.

Since we have been through this hustle, we understand the problems you come across this process. we bridge the gap by updating students with relevant information and statistics to help them in making the most informed and successful admission decisions.

We give students an in-depth analysis of the most deserved universities and admission requirements in those universities. Among the foreign universities, there are only a small number of universities that are able to deliver required medical education to foreign students, so admission to a foreign university should be taken up very seriously and with important information in hand. A very low success rate in Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (Screening Test) stresses the need for the right selection of colleges and universities if one is serious to attain a medical degree recognized in India.

We genuinely guide students and parents about the pros and cons of medical education from foreign universities. We are the trusted consultancy of India that offers services and guidance in foreign and personal attention towards our people.
